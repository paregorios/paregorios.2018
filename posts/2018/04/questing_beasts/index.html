Questing Beasts

Tom Elliott

2018-04-08 15:46

Source

For those at home following my Hosting Quest, here are the suggested providers given me (so far) by friends and social media contacts:

  • AWS Lightsail
  • Cloudvault.me
  • Digital Ocean
  • EsoSoft
  • Linode
  • Mythic Beasts
  • Reclaim Hosting
  • Tiger Technologies
  • Vidahost
  • Webarchitects

There's a lot to like about all these recommendations; however, I'm going with Tiger Technologies for now. I like their combination of green power, Apache, free HTTPS over TLS using a certificate from Let's Encrypt, free domain name management and renewal, free WHOIS privacy, email, ssh/shell, and unmetered bandwidth.

I'll let you know how it goes.
